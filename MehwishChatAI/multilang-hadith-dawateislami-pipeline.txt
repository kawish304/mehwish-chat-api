✔️ Format: JSON/XML scraping or API-based readonly access

🔌 Integration Pipeline:

1. 🛡️ Connect to Dawat-e-Islami Hadith Section (readonly)
2. 📥 Scrape or access text in Urdu + Arabic (original)  
3. 🌐 Translate to other languages using verified scholarly translations (no GPT translation)
4. 📦 Store in a safe DB or `.json` format for multilingual query
5. ❌ Disallow user-based tafsir / AI interpretation

---

✅ Allowed Use-Cases in AI:

- 🔍 Search Hadith by keyword or book
- 🗣️ Respond with “According to Sahih Bukhari...” (readonly reference)
- 📘 Show Arabic + translation
- 🔇 No opinion, fatwa, or personal explanation

---

🚫 Disallowed:

- ❌ Fatwa generation
- ❌ Custom tafsir
- ❌ Editing hadith content
- ❌ GPT-based religious rulings
- ❌ Making own hadith summaries
